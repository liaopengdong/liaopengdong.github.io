Simple Invoice Workflow for Freelancers and Small Teams

A practical workflow for preparing invoice details, hourly totals, payment terms, tax notes, and client-facing copy.

Collect the billing details

Before generating an invoice, confirm client name, invoice date, due date, item descriptions, quantities, rates, discounts, and tax notes. Clear inputs reduce payment delays.

Check totals before sending

Use calculators for hourly totals, subtotal, discount, taxes, and payment fees. This is especially useful when a project includes both fixed and variable work.

Send a clear payment email

A short email with invoice number, due date, payment method, and next steps is easier for clients to act on than a long message with buried details.
